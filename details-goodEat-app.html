GoodEat

web application based on the Django framework of Python, designed for the management and visualization of food products. The basic functionalities are the search and visualization of products, accessible to non-registered visitors. For registered users, additional functionalities will be available. They will have the possibility to add new products, remove products from the database, and customize their experience by disabling the display of certain products. These functionalities will be implemented using Django's relational database management capabilities.
